How it Works

Save money, meet awesome people, and consume less
3galty is a premier bike rental service that provides a platform to connect with active people looking to rent or borrow in Egypt. Through our service, renters search for a bike in whatever area they want and complete the rental process easily through our website. We even offer various protection options and delivery services to make everyone happy.

How We Built Trust

ID Verification

Renters and listers verify their 3galty accounts with their Credit Card, mobile phone number, Twitter and Facebook. The verification validates your online identity to help us get a better idea of who is rent. Verifying your account helps others trust you and makes your ride show up higher in search results.

Community Reviews

We welcome all of our users to review each other following their rental to provide a resource that highlights everyone’s experiences on 3galty. These reviews help create a sense of community for our users and build a marketplace based on safety and trust.

Keep Your Privacy

We never share your information with anyone until you confirm their rental. You can keep all communications in our secure, private messaging system so you don't need to share your contact info.
